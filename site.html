MF DOOM, born Daniel Dumile, was a legendary underground rapper and producer known for his complex lyrics, unique flow, and iconic metal mask. His career began in the late 1980s with KMD, but he reemerged in the late '90s with a new identity inspired by the comic villain Doctor Doom. His debut solo album, Operation: Doomsday, introduced his dark, imaginative style.

With albums like Madvillainy (2004), a collaboration with producer Madlib, DOOM became a hip-hop icon, celebrated for his innovative sampling and intricate storytelling. Though he kept a low public profile, his influence on hip-hop remains profound.
